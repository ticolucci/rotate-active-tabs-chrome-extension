Change Keyboard Shortcut

Due to Chrome security restrictions, extensions cannot directly open the shortcuts settings page. Follow these simple steps to change your keyboard shortcuts:

How to Change the Shortcuts

  1. Click the "Copy URL" button below to copy the shortcuts page URL
  2. Paste the URL into your browser's address bar and press Enter
  3. Find "Rotate Active Tabs" in the list of extensions
  4. You'll see two shortcuts:
    • Rotate through active tabs - Forward rotation
    • Rotate through active tabs (reverse) - Backward rotation
  5. Click the pencil icon or the shortcut field next to each command
  6. Press your desired key combination (e.g., Alt+Q, Ctrl+Shift+Q, etc.)
  7. Your new shortcuts are saved automatically!
chrome://extensions/shortcuts
Note:

After changing the shortcuts, you can return to the Options page and click the "Refresh Shortcuts" button to see your updated shortcuts displayed.
